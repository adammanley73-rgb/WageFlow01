Who We Are

Built in Liverpool. Built for UK business.

The Business Consortium Ltd is a Liverpool-based software company founded by a disabled UK Armed Forces veteran.

We build compliance-first SaaS products for UK payroll and HR teams. Our tools are built to HMRC standards and designed for the people who run them every day.

We are not a large consultancy. We are a focused software company that does one thing well.

  • UK-focused, HMRC-aligned, built for the long term

Our Story

Why we built this.

I started The Business Consortium Ltd because I could not find payroll and HR tools that were honest about what they did and did not do. After leaving the Armed Forces, I spent years working with small businesses and bureaus who were let down by software that overpromised. WageFlow and PeopleFlow are my answer to that. Built properly, in Liverpool, with compliance at the centre.
Adam Manley
Founder, The Business Consortium Ltd
